# Anti-Slop Checklist

> Quick-reference version. Full guide with examples and word lists: [README.md](README.md)

## Before you publish, check:

**Words** — Search for [Tier 1](README.md#tier-1-kill-on-sight) words (kill all), [Tier 2](README.md#tier-2-suspicious-in-clusters) clusters (3+ in a paragraph = rewrite), and [filler phrases](README.md#filler-phrases) (delete all). Or run `./slop-check.py yourfile.md`.

**Sentences** — First word of every sentence a transition? Rewrite. All sentences 15-25 words? Mix lengths. Hedging chains ("may potentially")? State it or don't.

**Paragraphs** — Same template repeating? Break it. All the same length? Vary them. No voice? Add one.

**Structure** — Sections suspiciously balanced? Lump them. List abuse? Convert to prose. Throat-clearing opener? Cut it. "In conclusion" ending? End with your actual point.

**Smell test** — Read it aloud. Does it sound like a person or a press release? Could you swap the topic and it still works? Is there a single surprising sentence?
